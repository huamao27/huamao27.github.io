AI-Enhanced ESG Reporting

Dr Huamao Wang | Associate Professor in Finance, Risk and Banking

Nottingham University Business School | University of Nottingham

Research funded by British Academy Mid-Career Fellowship (MCFSS25_250036)

📊 Research Overview

Investigating how AI can support SMEs in adopting AI-enhanced ESG reporting to meet emerging regulatory requirements and supply chain demands.

Sample: 1,000 UK SMEs across 13 sectors and 4 size bands

Frameworks: IFRS S1/S2, GRI Standards, VSME

🔍 Key Findings

  • AI can generate reasonable draft ESG reports from public company data
  • Professional advisor review adds credibility value
  • Hallucination rates below 3%
  • SME ESG data exists, and the gap is framework understanding
