AI-Enhanced ESG Reporting

British Academy Fellowship Research | University of Nottingham

📊 Research Overview

Investigating how AI can support SMEs in adopting AI-enhanced ESG reporting to meet emerging regulatory requirements and supply chain demands.

Sample: 1,000 UK SMEs across 13 sectors and 4 size bands

Frameworks: IFRS S1/S2, GRI Standards, VSME

🔍 Key Findings

  • AI can generate reasonable draft ESG reports from public company data
  • Professional advisor review adds 30-40% credibility value
  • Framework switching is expensive (low vocabulary overlap)
  • SME ESG data exists—the gap is framework understanding
